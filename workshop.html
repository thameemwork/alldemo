BOB MOESTA

Co-founder and CEO of the Rewired Group, Co-Architect of the Jobs-to-be-Done framework

Join author and innovator Bob Moesta in this workshop focused on the core skills essential to innovate and build products and businesses.

Through his decades of experience developing over 3500 products and services, Bob has distilled his knowledge into five core skills essential for innovation. This four-hour workshop will not only introduce you to these skills but will provide you an opportunity to reflect on and learn from your own prior experience.

We’ll begin with an introduction to the five skills, which include:

- Uncovering demand
- Empathetic perspective
- Causal structures
- Prototyping to learn
- Making tradeoffs

As you learn more about each skill, you’ll gain practical frameworks and processes you can apply in your own life and work. You’ll also hear real-world examples of how others like you have learned and implemented these skills to make progress in their approach to business. In addition, this workshop will include ample time for you to reflect and apply these skills to your own products, services, systems and businesses.

Ultimately, you will leave the workshop with a grasp of these five skills and how you can leverage them to make your own progress. This workshop is perfect for Product Managers, Technology Entrepreneurs, and anybody else involved in bringing innovative products to life.
