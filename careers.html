Location:Enviro Analysts & Engineers Pvt. Ltd.
Department:EIA
Reporting Manager:GM- EIA/ Vice President
Experience:4-7 Years
Industry:Preferred from real estate / Envt. consultancy / Industry Background

Minimum experience for FAEs

  • For category A FAEs (see Section 9 below) minimum 5 years overall experience in the functional area, of which at least 3 years should be EIA related
  • For Category B FAEs minimum 3 years overall experience in the functional area of which at least 1 year should be EIA related#.(Vide Appendix A for details).

#. AND INITIMATION AS A TEAM MEMBER SHOULD BE DONE FROM THAT ORGANIZATION

Educational qualifications specific to functional area

i. Engineering- Environmental / Chemical / Mechanical / Civil / Mining / Chemical technology,

ii. Master's (post graduate) degree in Science subjects / Environmental Sciences / Environment Management / Environmental Studies / Industrial Pollution Control / Chemical Technology / Physics / Chemistry and the like.

Experience specific to functional area:

i. Air pollution monitoring

  • Meteorology - measurement of wind speed, wind direction, relative humidity, wet and dry bulb temperature, rainfall etc.
  • Air quality monitoring â€“ selecting locations of sampling stations, monitoring/ supervision of monitoring of suspended and respirable particulate matter (PM2.5 and PM 10), SO2, NOx, CO, hydrocarbons and other relevant parameters as per NAAQ standards, including handling of relevant equipment, collection and preservation of the samples
  • Stack monitoring - particulate matter, SO2, NOx, and other relevant parameters
  • Inventorization of greenhouse gas emissions

ii. Air pollution prevention and control

  • Reduction in process emissions at source
  • Reduction in fugitive emissions

  • Identification of probable impacts of the different air emissions from the plants/facilities proposed

  • Knowledge/experience on design consideration of air pollution control systems and their efficiencies

  • Adequate knowledge of EIA Legislation process in India

  • Understanding of policies, guidelines and the legislation related to air pollution
  • Knowledge of international treaties related to emissions and preparing cost
