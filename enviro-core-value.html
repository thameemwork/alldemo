Core Values

To be the final authority on legal and compliance issues related to the Group's activities.

To engage competent professionals, duly approved by the regulatory or competent authorities.

To be socially responsible for prevention of pollution and environmental protection, occupational health, safety and quality in all our environmental surveys, studies, projects, monitoring and testing.

To strive for excellence in all our activities through continuous improvement in systems and employee development.

To provide quality products to our customers on time through continual improvement to enhance consumer satisfaction.

To provide a comprehensive framework for monitoring our performance and progress against objectives and targets.

Living Our Belief

For the Enviro Group, CSR is a mindset and a way of life. It is the very basis of our entire business proposition. We have chosen a profession which necessarily involves protection of the environment by upholding the laws of the land. However, our intent goes far beyond; it gives us satisfaction to ensure that these laws are followed in spirit, not just in letter.

Without drawing a distinct line between our professional and CSR activities, we have spearheaded numerous tree plantation drives, implemented drinking water treatment and undertaken various other actions that ensure sustainability of the environment. It is our belief that we are an inseparable part of our ecosystem and any man-made damage that is done to the environment is effectively done to the perpetrator of that damage too. We spread this message to those we interact with towards ensuring a more sustainable tomorrow.
